# The Viking Project

**The world has been invaded by a relentless, football-crazed Viking.**
He is stealing the joy of the beautiful game, and chaos has spread across the globe. You are humanity's last hope. You must lead your team through an intense global tournament, facing off against 10 nations and outsmarting the Viking to restore peace to the world!

## How to Play

### Controls
* **Pass the ball:** Click on a teammate, or press the number keys **1, 2, 3, 4, or 5**.
* **Shoot the ball:** Click on the opponent's goal, or press the **Spacebar**.
* **Start the Game:** Press **S**.
* **Pause the Game:** Press **P**.
* **Restart the Game:** Press **R**.

### Rules of the Pitch
1. **Keep Away from the Viking:** The Viking will constantly chase whoever has the ball. If he touches the player with the ball, you lose a life!
2. **Watch the Defenders:** As you progress through the levels, enemy defenders will spawn. Passing or shooting near them significantly increases the chance of an interception.
3. **Patience is Key:** Holding the ball for a few seconds will slightly increase your pass and shot accuracy, but don't hold it too long or the Viking will catch you!
4. **Pass Chains:** Passing to the immediate next player in the numerical sequence (e.g., Player 2 to Player 3) gives a bonus to pass accuracy.
5. **Get on Fire:** If a player receives a perfect pass, they might catch "on fire," giving them a 100% chance of completing their next action and stunning the Viking briefly.

## How to Beat the Game
To achieve victory and restore world peace, you must **score a goal in all 10 rounds**.
* Each round represents a different nation, getting progressively harder as the Viking gets faster and more defenders appear on the pitch.
* You start with 5 lives. You lose a life if a pass is intercepted, if a shot misses, or if the Viking tackles you.
* Once you beat the 10th round, you will be crowned World Champion and can submit your score to the global leaderboard!

Good luck, and save the world!
