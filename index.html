How to Play

  • Pass the ball by clicking teammates or pressing 1-5 to avoid the Viking.
  • Enemy defenders decrease your pass and shoot success probabilities.
  • When a player is "On Fire", their passes and shots have a 100% success rate!
  • Click the opponent goal or press Space to shoot and score.
  • Win 10 rounds to become the World Champion!
